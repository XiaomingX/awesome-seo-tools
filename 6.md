# Open Source Marketing Campaign & Attribution

## Marketing Campaign Management & Dashboards
- **[Campaign Dashboard](https://github.com/topics/campaign-dashboard)**: Various projects exist for visualizing campaign performance.
- **[Apache Superset](https://github.com/apache/superset)**: A modern, enterprise-ready business intelligence web application. Excellent for building custom cross-channel marketing dashboards.
- **[PostHog](https://github.com/PostHog/posthog)**: While primarily product analytics, its correlation analysis and experimentation features make it powerful for measuring campaign effectiveness on product growth.

## Marketing Attribution Modeling
- **[Marketing Attribution Models (DP6)](https://github.com/DP6/Marketing-Attribution-Models)**: Python library implementing various attribution models (Last Click, Time Decay, Shapley Value, Markov Chains).
- **[OpenAttribution](https://github.com/OpenAttribution/open-attribution)**: Open-source Mobile Measurement Platform (MMP) for tracking ad spend and ROI.
- **[Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM)](https://github.com/facebookexperimental/Robyn)**: (e.g., Facebook's Robyn, though check license) or other open implementations for analyzing channel contribution to sales.

## Marketing Automation & Tracking
- **[Mautic](https://github.com/mautic/mautic)**: Review open Source Marketing Automation Project. It tracks contacts and campaigns, providing built-in reporting on email and landing page performance.
- **[OpenPanel](https://github.com/OpenPanel-dev/OpenPanel)**: A comprehensive analytics tool for tracking user journeys and funnels, useful for attribution.
