# Open Source AIO & AEO Tools

## AI Search & Answer Engine Optimization (AIO/AEO)
Tools to help content appear in AI overviews (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google SGE).

- **[GetCito](https://github.com/ai-search-guru/getcito-worlds-first-open-source-aio-aeo-or-geo-tool)**: The world's first open-source tool dedicated to AIO/AEO and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO).
- **[AutoGEO](https://github.com/topics/generative-engine-optimization)**: Automated framework to optimize content for generative search engines, aiming for higher visibility in AI answers.
- **[AI Monitor](https://github.com/topics/generative-engine-optimization)**: Tracks brand mentions across AI platforms like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.
- **[OptimðŸ‘€ (AEO Site)](https://github.com/chikodilee/aeo-site)**: Analyzes websites against 20+ AEO factors to score and recommend improvements for AI visibility.
- **[LLM Brand Tracker](https://github.com/sarahkb125/llm-brand-tracker)**: Monitors how your brand is perceived and cited by Large Language Models.

## Schema & Structured Data for LLMs
Helping LLMs understand your content structure.

- **[JSON Schema Builder & AI Generator](https://github.com/amir9480/json-schema-builder)**: Visual tool with AI integration to generate schemas, helping structure data for AI parsers.
- **[LLM4Schema.org](https://github.com/GDD-Nantes/LLM4SchemaOrg)**: Research and tools for generating valid Schema.org markup using LLMs.

## Content Optimization for LLMs
- **[ALwrity](https://github.com/alwrity/alwrity)**: AI-powered content creation toolkit that focuses on writing research-backed, AI-readable content.
- **[LangExtract](https://github.com/google/langextract)**: (Google) Library to extract structured data from unstructured text, useful for auditing how LLMs might parse your pages.
