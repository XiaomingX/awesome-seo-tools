# Enterprise and Industry SaaS

## Call Center & Contact Center Systems
- **[VICIdial](https://github.com/vicidial/vicidial)**: Most popular open-source contact center globally. Features predictive dialing and inbound/outbound support.
- **[GOautodial](https://github.com/goautodial/goautodial)**: Modern omni-channel contact center suite based on VICIdial with a user-friendly interface.
- **[OMniLeads](https://github.com/OmniLeads/OMniLeads)**: WebRTC-based contact center for Asterisk.
- **[Issabel](https://github.com/IssabelFoundation/issabel)**: Unified communications platform integrating PBX, mailing, and collaboration.

## Hotel & Property Management (PMS)
- **[QloApps](https://github.com/Qloapps/QloApps)**: Popular open-source hotel reservation & booking system. Suitable for launching a hotel website.
- **[Odoo Hotel](https://github.com/odoo/odoo)**: Odoo can be configured as a comprehensive hotel management ERP.

## Restaurant & POS
- **[TastyIgniter](https://github.com/tastyigniter/TastyIgniter)**: Specialized open-source restaurant management platform (online ordering, reservations).
- **[Floreant POS](https://github.com/floreantpos/floreantpos)**: Platform-independent POS for restaurants.
- **[SambaPOS](https://github.com/Sambapos/SambaPOS)**: Legendary open-source restaurant POS codebase.
- **[Odoo POS](https://github.com/odoo/odoo)**: Excellent for restaurants and bars (floor/table management).

## Retail & Inventory Management
- **[Odoo Inventory/POS](https://github.com/odoo/odoo)**: The gold standard for open-source retail ERP.
- **[FluentPOS](https://github.com/fluentpos/fluentpos)**: Modern open-source POS built with Angular and ASP.NET Core.
- **[OpenSourcePOS](https://github.com/opensourcepos/opensourcepos)**: Web-based Point of Sale perfectly suited for retail.
- **[InvenTree](https://github.com/inventree/InvenTree)**: Powerful open-source inventory management system for parts and tracking.
