# Open Source Enterprise SaaS & Call Center Systems

## Call Center & Outbound Dialer
- **[VICIdial](https://github.com/vicidial/vicidial)**: The most popular open-source contact center globally. Features predictive dialing, inbound/outbound support, and agent scripting.
- **[GOautodial](https://github.com/goautodial/goautodial)**: Modern extensive open source omni-channel contact center suite (based on VICIdial) with a user-friendly interface.
- **[OMniLeads](https://github.com/OmniLeads/OMniLeads)**: WebRTC-based contact center for Asterisk. Designed for easy administration and management.
- **[Issabel](https://github.com/IssabelFoundation/issabel)**: Unified communications platform integrating PBX, mailing, and collaboration tasks.

## Other Worthy Enterprise SaaS to Study
- **[ERPNext](https://github.com/frappe/erpnext)**: A fully featured, modern, open-source ERP system. Great for studying complex business logic.
- **[Odoo](https://github.com/odoo/odoo)**: A suite of web-based open source business apps (Sales, CRM, PoS, Website, etc.).
- **[Chatwoot](https://github.com/chatwoot/chatwoot)**: Open-source customer engagement suite. An alternative to Intercom, Zendesk, Salesforce Service Cloud.
- **[Helpy](https://github.com/helpyio/helpy)**: Modern, mobile-first helpdesk customer support software.
