# Marketing Analytics and Attribution

## Influencer Marketing Platforms
- **[HypeIt](https://github.com/Ismriti12/HypeIt-Influencer-Marketing-Platform)**: A web-based platform for organizing influencer marketing campaigns and discovering influencers.
- **[Worfinsta](https://github.com/amitascra/worfinsta)**: Django-based influencer marketing platform connecting brands and influencers.
- **[Influencer Brand Network](https://github.com/shaunthecomputerscientist/Influencer_Brand-Network)**: Connects influencers/businesses with AI-driven insights and campaign management.

## Social Media Analytics Dashboards
- **[InstaTrack](https://github.com/topics/instagram-tracker)**: Tools for tracking Instagram followers and engagement.
- **[TikTok Analytics](https://github.com/topics/tiktok-analytics)**: Projects for visualizing trending videos and creator stats.
- **[YouTube Analytics Dashboard](https://github.com/topics/youtube-analytics)**: Dashboards to visualize channel performance and subscriber metrics.

## Marketing Campaign Management & Dashboards
- **[Apache Superset](https://github.com/apache/superset)**: Modern, enterprise-ready BI web application. Excellent for custom cross-channel marketing dashboards.
- **[PostHog](https://github.com/PostHog/posthog)**: While primarily product analytics, its correlation analysis and experimentation features make it powerful for measuring campaign effectiveness.

## Marketing Attribution & Modeling
- **[Marketing Attribution Models (DP6)](https://github.com/DP6/Marketing-Attribution-Models)**: Python library implementing various attribution models (Last Click, Time Decay, Shapley Value, Markov Chains).
- **[OpenAttribution](https://github.com/OpenAttribution/open-attribution)**: Open-source Mobile Measurement Platform (MMP) for tracking ad spend and ROI.
- **[Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM)](https://github.com/facebookexperimental/Robyn)**: Frameworks (like Facebook's Robyn) for analyzing channel contribution to sales.

## Marketing Automation & Tracking
- **[Mautic](https://github.com/mautic/mautic)**: Open Source Marketing Automation Project. Tracks contacts and campaigns with built-in reporting.
- **[OpenPanel](https://github.com/OpenPanel-dev/OpenPanel)**: Comprehensive analytics tool for tracking user journeys and funnels.
