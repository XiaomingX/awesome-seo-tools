# Open Source Influencer & Social Media Metrics

## Influencer Marketing Platforms
- **[HypeIt](https://github.com/Ismriti12/HypeIt-Influencer-Marketing-Platform)**: A web-based platform for organizing influencer marketing campaigns and discovering influencers.
- **[Worfinsta](https://github.com/amitascra/worfinsta)**: Django-based influencer marketing platform connecting brands and influencers.
- **[Influencer Brand Network](https://github.com/shaunthecomputerscientist/Influencer_Brand-Network)**: Connects influencers/businesses with AI-driven insights and campaign management.

## Social Media Analytics Dashboards
### Instagram
- **[InstaTrack](https://github.com/topics/instagram-tracker)** (Generic Topic): Various tools exist for tracking followers and engagement.
- **[Instagram Analytics](https://github.com/topics/instagram-analytics)**: Search topic for dashboard projects, often using Python/Streamlit.

### TikTok
- **[TikTok Analytics](https://github.com/topics/tiktok-analytics)**: Projects often use unofficial APIs or data exports to visualize trending videos and creator stats using Streamlit/Plotly.

### YouTube
- **[YouTube Analytics Dashboard](https://github.com/topics/youtube-analytics)**: Many Python-based dashboards (Streamlit/Plotly) exist to visualize channel performance, subscribers, and view metrics using the YouTube Data API.

## Advanced & Data Science
- **[Admass](https://github.com/pshetty44/Admass)**: Project for identifying and categorizing influencer groups on Instagram.
- **[Marketing Mix Modeling](https://github.com/viznuv/influencer_social_media_MMM)**: Framework for analyzing the ROI and sales impact of influencer marketing.
