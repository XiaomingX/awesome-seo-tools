# Open Source Hotel, Restaurant & Retail SaaS

## Hotel & Property Management (PMS)
- **[QloApps](https://github.com/Qloapps/QloApps)**: Very popular open-source hotel reservation & booking system. Suitable for launching a hotel website.
- **[Qldt](https://github.com/topics/hotel-management-system)**: Various hotel management system projects exist on GitHub (keywords: Hotel Management System).
- **[Odoo Hotel](https://github.com/odoo/odoo)**: Odoo can be configured as a comprehensive hotel management ERP.

## Restaurant Management & POS
- **[TastyIgniter](https://github.com/tastyigniter/TastyIgniter)**: specialized open-source restaurant management platform (online ordering, reservations).
- **[Floreant POS](https://github.com/floreantpos/floreantpos)**: A platform-independent POS for restaurants (Touch screen friendly).
- **[SambaPOS](https://github.com/Sambapos/SambaPOS)**: Though V3 is old, it's a legendary open-source restaurant POS codebase.
- **[Odoo POS](https://github.com/odoo/odoo)**: The POS module in Odoo is excellent for restaurants and bars (floor/table management).

## Retail & Inventory Management
- **[Odoo Inventory/POS](https://github.com/odoo/odoo)**: The gold standard for open-source retail ERP. Best for serious businesses.
- **[FluentPOS](https://github.com/fluentpos/fluentpos)**: Modern open-source POS built with Angular and ASP.NET Core.
- **[OpenSourcePOS](https://github.com/opensourcepos/opensourcepos)**: Web-based Point of Sale perfectly suited for retail.
- **[InvenTree](https://github.com/inventree/InvenTree)**: Powerful open-source inventory management system for parts and tracking.
