# Surveys and Collaborative Databases

## Survey & Form Builders
- **[Typebot](https://github.com/baptisteArno/typebot.io)**: Powerful visual builder for conversational forms and surveys. High engagement rates for marketing lead gen.
- **[Formbricks](https://github.com/formbricks/formbricks)**: Open-source Qualtrics alternative. Great for product experience management and in-app micro-surveys.
- **[LimeSurvey](https://github.com/LimeSurvey/LimeSurvey)**: The industry standard for complex, academic-grade surveys.
- **[SurveyJS](https://github.com/surveyjs/survey-library)**: A JavaScript Form Library to build your own survey platform.

## Multidimensional Tables (Airtable Alternatives)
- **[NocoDB](https://github.com/nocodb/nocodb)**: Turns any SQL database into a smart spreadsheet. A self-hosted Airtable alternative.
- **[Baserow](https://github.com/bram2w/baserow)**: Open source no-code database and Airtable alternative. User-friendly and scalable.
- **[APITable](https://github.com/apitable/apitable)**: API-oriented open-source low-code platform for building collaborative apps.
- **[Grist](https://github.com/gristlabs/grist-core)**: Hybrid of a spreadsheet and database, highly customizable for data-driven marketing workflows.
